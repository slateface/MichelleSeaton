Michelle the actor...

Michelle was last seen in the Off Off Broadway production of Us vs. Them. Some other acting roles include Aphrodite in Cupid and Psyche, Andromache in Trojan Women, Frog in Frogwoman, Lenore in Treatment, Viola in Twelfth Night, Mary in Serenading Louie. Michelle trained as an actor/director for three years at Mason Gross of the Arts. She has studied with Deborah Hedwall, William Carden, Pamela Berlin in acting, John Basil in Shakespeare and Lenard Petit in Clown. She won a “Best Ensemble” drammy for Electropuss, which she directed. Michelle holds a MFA in Directing from Rutgers, Mason Gross School of the Arts, is a graduate of Portland Actors Conservatory and holds a BA in Theatre from Linfield College.
