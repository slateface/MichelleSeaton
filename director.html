Let an award winning Director work for you...

Michelle Seaton is an award winning director who has recently moved back to Portland, Oregon after living in New York/New Jersey for the past 8 years. During her stay there she founded and served as the Artistic Director for Dark Luna Productions, a theatre company that seeks to address social and political issue while promoting diversity. In 2009, Michelle graduated from Mason Gross School of the Arts, Rutgers University with an MFA in Directing. Some NY/NJ directing credits include Off Off Broadway Us Vs. Them and Passage. Other east coast directing credits include Egyptology, Walking Shadows, Cloud Tectonics, Voir Dire, Dog Sees God, Stop Kiss, Wilde Abandon and Rosemary with Ginger. Some other directing credits include Drammy award winning ElectroPuss, Kennedy’s Center Merit of Direction UMW, The House of Bernarda Alba, Weeping Woman, Thrust and The Flu Season. She has directed over 25 new plays and has worked in theatres such as Ensemble Studio Theatre, St. Louis Repertory, The Eugene O’Neill Center and the American Globe Theatre. Prior to graduate school Michelle was the Resident Director and Actor for Stark Raving Theatre in Portland, OR. Michelle is a graduate from Portland Actors Conservatory and holds a double BA in Theatre and Communication from Linfield College.
